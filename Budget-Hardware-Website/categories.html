Our Range

Every material, for every build.

Category 01

Power Tools

Professional-grade power tools from the world's leading manufacturers. From precision drills to heavy-duty circular saws, our range covers every cutting, fastening, and finishing task. All stocked by certified tradespeople who can advise on the right tool for your application.

Category 02

Building Materials

Structural and finishing materials sourced from certified suppliers. Cement, block, reinforced steel, insulation, cladding, and more. Our bulk supply program offers competitive pricing with same-week delivery for large project orders.

Category 03

Electrical Supplies

A comprehensive range of electrical components for residential and commercial installations. Cable, conduit, distribution boards, lighting, and smart home systems. All products meet current safety regulations and come with full technical documentation.

Category 04

Plumbing Supplies

Full plumbing system components from pipe and fittings to valves, pumps, and water heaters. We supply both contractors installing new systems and homeowners managing repairs — with expert staff on hand to guide complex specifications.

Category 05

Paints & Finishes

Interior and exterior paints, primers, varnishes, and specialty coatings. Our in-store mixing service matches any color across major palette systems. Whether you're finishing a bedroom or weatherproofing a facade, we carry the right product for the surface.

Category 06

Safety Equipment

Site safety is non-negotiable. We supply helmets, harnesses, gloves, eye protection, high-visibility workwear, and fall arrest systems. All equipment meets current safety standards and is priced for teams ordering in bulk.
