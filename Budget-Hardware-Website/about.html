Our Story

Three decades of building the builders.

Mission

We exist to empower every builder.

Budget Hardware was founded in 1992 with a single conviction: that professionals deserve access to the same quality materials that large construction firms take for granted.

Today, we supply over 8,000 contractors, architects, and builders across the region with hardware, materials, and expertise that elevate every project.

Quality First
Every product is vetted by our team. We only carry what we'd use ourselves.
Expert Guidance
Our staff are tradespeople, not just salespeople. Real knowledge, real advice.
Deep Inventory
12,000+ products in stock. If we don't have it, we'll source it.
Local Roots
Community-first since day one. We grow when our builders grow.
1992 Founded in Maseru with a 500m² warehouse and a team of four.
2001 Expanded to three locations and launched our contractor account program.
2010 Reached 5,000 active professional accounts. Launched fleet delivery service.
2018 Opened flagship 4,000m² warehouse. Became authorized Bosch & Makita dealer.
2024 12,000+ products across 6 locations. Still family-owned. Still quality-first.
